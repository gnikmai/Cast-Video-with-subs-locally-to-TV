Instructions


  • Make sure that your TV/device and PC are connected to the same Wi-Fi network

  • Chrome/Edge setting (the three dots)

  • More tools > Cast media to device

  • Choose the TV/device to cast to

  • Select Sources > Cast tab

  • Enable fullscreen mode on the video player

  • Now you can watch movies with subtitles on the big screen without a Chromecast device or USB
